Iandra is a site with a historic building, Mt Oriel. For more information on
Iandra, please see [Greenethorpe Public School's](https://web.archive.org/web/2008/http://www.greenethor-p.schools.nsw.edu.au/Pages/Local%20History.htm) and [NSW Heritage Office's](https://web.archive.org/web/2008/http://www.heritage.nsw.gov.au/07_subnav_02_2.cfm?itemid=5051843) web pages.

Apparently there was also a [Railway Station](https://web.archive.org/web/2008/http://www.nswrail.net/lines/grenfell/greenethorpe.html) nearby that has since been closed. Also see the [Grenfell](https://web.archive.org/web/2005/http://www.grenfell.org.au/tourism/grenfellsurrounds.htm) page.
