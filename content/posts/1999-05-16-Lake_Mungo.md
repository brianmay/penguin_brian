Lake Mungo is a dried up lake that has special significance since some very old
bones have been found.

For more information, refer to the [Lake Mungo Web Page](https://web.archive.org/web/2007/https://www.npws.nsw.gov.au/parks/outback/out003.html) maintained by [NSW National Parks and Wildlife Service](https://web.archive.org/web/2007/https://www.npws.nsw.gov.au/).
